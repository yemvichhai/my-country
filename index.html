CAMBODIA

City

Phnom Penh

Phnom Penh is the capital and largest city of Cambodia, located at the confluence of three rivers: the Mekong, Tonlé Sap, and Bassac.

It's the cultural, political, and economic heart of the country, with a rich history and a rapidly modernizing skyline.

People

17.8 million

As of 2025, Cambodia's population is approximately 17.8 million, with a median age of 26.2 years.

The country is experiencing a youthful demographic, with about 65% of the population under the age of 30.

Currency

Reil

Cambodia's official currency is the Cambodian Riel (KHR), abbreviated as ៛.

However, the U.S. Dollar (USD) is widely accepted and often used alongside the riel for most transactions.

Food

Cambodian cuisine is known for its blend of sweet, sour, salty, and bitter flavors, often featuring rice, fish, and fresh vegetables.

Language

The official language of Cambodia is Khmer, also known as Cambodian.

Khmer is an Austroasiatic language spoken by the Khmer people and is the native language for a majority of the population in Cambodia.
